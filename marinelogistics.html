MARINE LOGISTICS SERVICES

Whitegravel Nigeria limited maintains a variety of construction Barges that are available on the Niger-Delta waterways. We also have Barges for hauling equipment such as Cranes ,pipes, pumps and tanks. We have spud Barges, which can easily support a large crane and can be utilized as a conveyor or Loading platform.
Whitegravel Nigeria limited has Tugboats available for onshore towing & Contract leasing. Our Tugboats are maintained to the highest level and undergo annual inspection by trained officers to ensure that our policies and safety procedures are adhered to.
